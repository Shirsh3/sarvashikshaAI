How to set up your sheet

  1. Click Copy Student Sheet Template above to get a ready-made template in your Google Drive.
  2. Open the sheet and fill in your students' names, grades, and any notes about their strengths and weaknesses.
  3. In the Active column, write Yes for students currently attending and No for those who left.
  4. Come back here, select the sheet from the dropdown, and click Save & Sync Students.
